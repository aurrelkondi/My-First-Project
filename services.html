#MotivatedByFitnessMAT17

Fitness MAT17 is the leading national premium fitness provider in Germany, operating more than 60 gyms in Germany. It offers its customers a comprehensive fitness and wellness experience with innovative training concepts and professional training support.

Fitness MAT17 supports its members in achieving their personal goals, regardless of age or performance level. It offers a broad range of training opportunities in the club, outdoors and online with the supplementary training features of its own NewMoove App. Fitness First is also the pioneer of functional training in Germany, a trend-setter in the fitness industry.

A membership at Fitness MAT17 offers further benefits, such as the complimentary use of the spacious wellness areas in each club, free child care for parents, and the opportunity for all members to bring a friend for free on Fridays to join them for their workout.

Fitness MAT17 also cooperates with more than 2,000 companies in Germany in order to strengthen corporate fitness and health management, thus helping people live healthier lives.
